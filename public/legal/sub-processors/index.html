GDPR Article 28 compliance · Last updated: April 2, 2026 · Version 1.0

GDPR Article 28 Notice: Under GDPR Article 28, PrimeCore Intelligence S.A. (Data Processor) is required to maintain a list of all sub-processors used in delivering the Services. Clients acting as Data Controllers are entitled to object to new sub-processors with 14 days' notice. PrimeCore will notify registered clients of any sub-processor changes via email before they take effect (see Section 5b tiered notice in Terms of Service). To be notified of changes, ensure your account email is current. To object to a sub-processor, contact privacy@primecoreintelligence.com.

Client Rights Under GDPR Article 28

As a Data Controller using PrimeCore Intelligence services, you have the right to:

  • Receive advance notice of new or changed sub-processors (see Section 5b tiered notice in Terms of Service)
  • Object to a new sub-processor within 14 days of notice — contact privacy@primecoreintelligence.com
  • Terminate the Agreement without penalty if a new sub-processor cannot be accommodated and PrimeCore cannot provide the Services without that sub-processor
  • Request a Data Processing Agreement (DPA) with Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) for your specific deployment
  • Audit or inspect sub-processor arrangements with reasonable notice
