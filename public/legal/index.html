AI Disclosure & Limitations
AIRequired reading for all contact center operators deploying PrimeCore Intelligence
Important: PrimeCore Intelligence is an AI-powered platform. AI outputs are not a substitute for professional legal, medical, financial, or compliance advice. Human oversight is required for all high-stakes decisions.

What the AI Does

The PrimeCore Intelligence AI system:

  • Processes spoken language and generates text/voice responses autonomously
  • Classifies caller intent and routes calls based on configured policy rules
  • Provides real-time suggestions and scripts to human agents via the Teleprompter overlay
  • Calculates performance metrics (FCR, AHT, SLA compliance) from call data
  • Operates autonomously on Tier-1 and Tier-2 call types as configured in your deployment

Known Limitations

The AI system has the following documented limitations:

  • Accuracy is not 100%. AI-generated responses may be incorrect, incomplete, or contextually inappropriate. Human review is recommended for complex or sensitive interactions.
  • Context window limitations. Very long or complex multi-part conversations may exceed the AI’s effective context window, leading to loss of earlier context.
  • Language nuance. While 50+ languages are supported, regional dialects, heavy accents, and specialized terminology may reduce accuracy. Performance varies by language and call type.
  • Real-time data. The AI does not have access to live external databases (e.g., live shipment GPS, live account balances) unless explicitly integrated via the API.
  • Hallucination risk. Like all large language models, the AI may generate plausible-sounding but factually incorrect information. Our 7-layer hallucination prevention system reduces but does not eliminate this risk.

AI Identity Disclosure

PrimeCore Intelligence’s AI systems are configured to acknowledge being an AI if directly and sincerely asked by a caller. We do not instruct our AI to claim to be human under any circumstances. This is a non-negotiable design requirement. Clients are responsible for ensuring callers are informed of AI interaction in accordance with applicable laws in their jurisdiction, including but not limited to: California’s BOT Disclosure Act; applicable TCPA provisions; any local AI or automated calling regulations in their country or state of operation.

Prohibited Uses

The PrimeCore Intelligence AI must not be used as the sole decision-maker, without human review or oversight, for:

  • Medical diagnoses, treatment recommendations, or emergency medical triage
  • Legal advice, legal document drafting, or legal representation
  • Financial investment advice or credit decisions
  • Mental health assessments or crisis intervention (human escalation is mandatory)
  • Age verification or identity verification for legally age-restricted products or services
  • Any decision with material legal consequences for an individual
PrimeCore Intelligence’s policy engine enforces mandatory human escalation paths for all crisis-related calls. These paths cannot be disabled by the client operator.

Call Recording Consent

PrimeCore Intelligence provides the technical infrastructure for call recording. The responsibility for obtaining legally required consent from callers lies entirely with the client operator. Requirements vary significantly by jurisdiction. Some examples: in the United States, many states require all-party consent for call recording; in the EU, consent is typically required under GDPR; in Brazil, call recording is governed by the Lei Geral de Proteção de Dados (LGPD); in Colombia and Mexico, equivalent consumer protection frameworks apply. PrimeCore Intelligence does not provide legal advice on consent requirements. Consult qualified legal counsel in each jurisdiction where you operate.

AI Improvement & Training

By default, anonymized and aggregated call patterns (never raw recordings, never personally identifiable data, never company-identifiable data) may be used to improve PrimeCore Intelligence models. You may opt out of this at any time by contacting privacy@primecoreintelligence.com. Opt-out does not affect service delivery. Individual call recordings are never used for training without explicit written consent.
